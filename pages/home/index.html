What is Temperate?

With our vast array of features available to anybody, healthcare will be more accessible, anywhere.

Digitized Data

You and your doctor will be able to deal away with handwritten paper records. Instead, digitized user IDs will store medical data for you.

Personalized Services

You would be able to personalize what services you would use. From one-to-one communication to our consultation systems, you can prioritize which doctor you would want to contact.

Accessibility is Key

With our unique application running on pure PHP and server-based HTML, our web application could be accessed from anywhere.

Dedicated Consultation

With our one-to-one consultation service, our application would enable you to send reports on your sickness and your doctors would be able to advice you and send back digitized receipts for your medications.

It's healthcare. More accessible. More efficient.

As the world changes, so must the way we do things change.

It's the future of healthcare. We are integrating healthcare with technology and business.
In Bitscript, we value your privacy.

With Temperate's secure code-reading service, all your data and medical records are stored securely in our server and locally, as a backup. Since you and your doctors rely on these records to be able to prescribe the medications and see your medical background as a whole, backups are done every 48 hours.

How do I consult my doctor with Temperate?

With our one-to-one personalized consultation service, you, as a patient would be able to report what you feel, send pictures and input your symptoms. To ensure that your doctor would be able to see your report immediately after they submit it, our application is equipped with push notifications. Then, your doctor would be able to send back their suggestions, analysis of your sickness and send you a digitized receipt of what medication you need.
